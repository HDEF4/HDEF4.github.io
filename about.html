Getting to Armagh



Note: Links to transport websites not yet added.
Northern Ireland can be reached by air from many cities in Britain, mainland Europe and beyond, and by ferry from England or Scotland. Belfast has two airports, Belfast International Airport and Belfast City Airport, both are ~40 miles from Armagh. It is also possible to travel to Northern Ireland via Dublin Airport in the Republic of Ireland, which is ~80 miles from Armagh. Note, however, that a separate visa may be required for certain nationals. While further from Armagh, a direct (but infrequent) connection exists between Armagh and Dublin Airport, unlike either of the Belfast airports.

From Belfast City or Belfast International Airport
A bus service operates from both airports to Belfast Europa Bus Centre. The Translink 300 service from Belfast International Airport operates between 05:00 and 00:00 and takes between 30 and 45 minutes to reach Belfast. The Translink 600 service from Belfast City Airport operates between 05:30 (07:30 on Sundays) and 21:50 and takes about 15 minutes.
The Ulsterbus Goldline services between Belfast Europa Buscentre and Armagh (250/251/270/271) take between 1 and 1.5 hours. Services typically operate between 09:00 and 20:00, although the frequency and operating hours of these services are less at weekends. Please consult the timetable before travelling.
Alternatively, Northern Ireland Railways operate rail services between Belfast city centre and Portadown. Portadown Railway Station is ~10 miles from Armagh. It is advisable to take a taxi from Portdown to Armagh as there are limited buses available.

From Dublin Airport
The Ulsterbus X4 service connects Dublin Airport direct to Armagh. There are only a small number of services each way, so it is advisable to consult the timetable before travelling. It is also possible to take a bus from Dublin Airport to Newry (X1 service) and then take a taxi to Armagh.
Northern Ireland Railways and Irish Rail jointly operate an express service between Belfast and Dublin city centre, stopping at Portadown. Portadown Railway Station is ~10 miles from Armagh. It is advisable to take a taxi from Portdown to Armagh as there are limited buses available.
